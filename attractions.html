Enjoy Floripa

The Island of the Magic

More than 40 beaches

Florianópolis is known as island of the magic, the capital of Santa Catarina is beautiful, sophisticated and unique, it attracts Brazilian and foreign tourists from various parts of the world to contemplate the exuberance of more than 40 beaches, and the historic and monumental sights that are part of of your beauty.

Sandboard at dunes

In the Dunes of Joaquina and Santinho, thrill seekers can venture with sandboarding, the adrenaline of surfing and enjoying the desert landscape that is almost impossible to imagine in this region of contrasts and colors, it is sensational.

Market of Florianópolis

In the city center is the Public Market built in 1898, where you will find shops, bars, live music and restaurants with the typical food of the region.

Kitesurf at Lagoa da Conceição

Lagoa da Conceição also has many surprises, Kitesurf is the favorite sport for visitors to this island’s largest lagoon.
